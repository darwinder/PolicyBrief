# EU Recovery Fund and Greek Fiscal Policy: Sustainability and Post-Funding Growth

### Overview
This is a research-based policy brief (1000-word) I completed in my second year of university, analysing Greece's fiscal strategy under the EU Recovery and Resilience Facility (NRRP: "Greece 2.0"). It evaluates whether the programme represents a structural shift in fiscal policy or a continuation of past reliance on external funding. It also assesses Greece's ability to sustain economic growth beyond 2026.
### Methodology & Tools
- Independent research & literature review
- Analysis of fiscal policy and macroeconomic indicators
- Use of secondary data from EU reports and international institutions
- Policy evaluation and critical comparison with historical fiscal approaches
- Visualization of data using MS Excel
